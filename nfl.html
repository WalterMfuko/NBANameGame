How to Play: NFL Players

Basic Rules:

1. The computer and you take turns naming NFL players

2. The first letter of your player's first name must match the first letter of the previous player's last name

Example:

- If the computer plays "Tom Brady"

- You must enter a player starting with "B" (like "Baker Mayfield")

- Then the computer will play a name starting with "M"

Hints:

You get 2 hints per turn. Click the hint button when needed.
