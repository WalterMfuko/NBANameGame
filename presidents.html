How to Play: US Presidents

Basic Rules:

1. The computer and you take turns naming US Presidents

2. The first letter of your president's first name must match the first letter of the previous president's last name

Example:

- If the computer plays "George Washington"

- You must enter a president starting with "W" (like "William Clinton")

- Then the computer will play a name starting with "C"

Hints:

You get 2 hints per turn. Click the hint button when needed.
