How to Play: Movies

Basic Rules:

1. The computer and you take turns naming Movies

2. The first letter of your movie title must match the last letter of the previous movie title

Example:

- If the computer plays "The Godfather"

- You must enter a movie starting with "R" (like "Rocky")

- Then the computer will play a movie starting with "Y"

Hints:

You get 2 hints per turn. Click the hint button when needed.
